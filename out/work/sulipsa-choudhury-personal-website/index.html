Sulipsa Choudhury Personal Website

View Website

Overview

I designed this website to create a soft editorial experience inspired by literary journals and storytelling spaces, moving away from standard commercial portfolios. My goal was to emotionally connect visitors with Sulipsa's poetry and performances while building professional credibility to support her collaboration and publishing opportunities.

My Design Approach & Strategy

The core UX challenge I faced was balancing emotional art with professional trust. Early concepts focused heavily on visual atmosphere, but I recognized this created a usability issue, visitors appreciated the aesthetic but missed her authority as a professional performer. To solve this, I designed a clear editorial system using serif typography, warm beige tones, and generous whitespace to ground the page structure.

Because her primary assets were performance videos rather than case studies, I structured a cinematic gallery that showcases her stage presence dynamically. I deliberately positioned her Social Diary lower to support, rather than distract from, her main literary identity, and added a dedicated book launch section to support organic audience growth.

My Outcomes

The final platform successfully balances artistic storytelling with professional credibility. By shifting the structure from a standard grid to a custom storytelling flow, I helped visitors engage with her performances while immediately understanding her availability for public projects, which established her value as an emerging performer and literary voice.
